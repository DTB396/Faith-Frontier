    <h1>The Faith Frontier Manifesto</h1>
    <p class="page-manifesto__subtitle ts-brass">A Forward Covenant of Truth · Equity · Faith</p>

  <article class="page-manifesto__body">
    <section id="vision" class="manifesto-block ts-emerald">
      <h2>Section 1: Our Vision for the Future</h2>
      <p>
        The Faith Frontier Ecclesiastical Trust advances a living future where faith, law, and community
        move together. We stand as an Everlasting Trust of Faith that prepares New Jersey for a new era of
        restoration, cultivating places where people live in one another’s shelter, and guiding society with
        the maxims of Truth · Equity · Faith.
      </p>
      <p>
        We see the Faith Frontier as a beacon for ethical progress: a sanctuary that equips leaders,
        neighbors, and institutions to build the common good with courage, clarity, and reverence. Our gaze is
        fixed on what we will plant—just governance, thriving communities, and resilient hope for generations to come.
      </p>
    </section>

    <section id="public-duty" class="manifesto-block ts-brass">
      <h2>Section 2: Our Sacred Duty to the Public</h2>
      <p>
        We hold a sacred trust to steward resources, decisions, and influence for the public benefit. As a
        disciplined ecclesiastical trust, we operate Extra Commercium—outside exploitation and aligned with the
        public good—so that every action serves peace, justice, and lawful order. We build trust through
        transparent governance, accountable leadership, and open communication with the people we serve.
      </p>
      <p>
        Our duty is a covenant of reliability: clear records, ethical compliance, and participation with civic
        partners to strengthen New Jersey’s institutions and the communities that rely on them.
      </p>
    </section>

    <section id="community-equity-faith" class="manifesto-block ts-emerald">
      <h2>Section 3: Our Commitments to Community, Equity, and Faith</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Community Restoration:</strong> Reweave local bonds through education, worship, mutual aid, and shared stewardship.</li>
        <li><strong>Equity in Action:</strong> Uphold dignified pathways for housing, health, employment, and civic participation, ensuring access and fairness for all.</li>
        <li><strong>Faith-Based Leadership:</strong> Lead with prayer, wisdom, and service that honor God and uplift every neighbor.</li>
        <li><strong>Public Trust Building:</strong> Invite community oversight, publish our measures of progress, and welcome collaboration.</li>
        <li><strong>Ethical Governance:</strong> Anchor decisions in law, conscience, and compassion, modeling integrity for all partners.</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <section id="stewardship" class="manifesto-block ts-brass">
      <h2>Section 4: Our Stewardship of Land, Work, and Renewal</h2>
      <ul>
        <li><strong>Land and Housing:</strong> Steward land with transparency, responsible development, and housing pathways that protect dignity and strengthen neighborhoods.</li>
        <li><strong>Workforce Renewal:</strong> Create vocational bridges, apprenticeships, and dignified jobs that prepare New Jersey’s people for resilient work.</li>
        <li><strong>Treatment and Rehabilitation:</strong> Expand faith-rooted care, behavioral health, and restorative services that help individuals heal and thrive.</li>
        <li><strong>Sustainable Infrastructure:</strong> Develop assets that respect creation, conserve resources, and serve communities for the long horizon.</li>
        <li><strong>Future Investment:</strong> Direct every asset—material and spiritual—toward long-term public benefit, guided by the enduring principle of Everlasting Trust of Faith.</li>
      </ul>
    </section>

    <section id="promise" class="manifesto-block ts-emerald">
      <h2>Section 5: Our Promise to New Jersey and All People We Serve</h2>
      <p>
        We promise to be a sanctuary for New Jersey: a trusted neighbor to every municipality, a partner to
        public servants, and a bridge to those seeking restoration. We will accompany communities in rebuilding
        homes, renewing faith, creating opportunity, and ensuring that every person can stand with dignity under
        just governance.
      </p>
      <p>
        Our promise is measurable—policies that are public, decisions that are documented, outcomes that are
        shared. We will build a legacy of transparency, equity, and service that future generations will inherit
        with confidence.
      </p>
    </section>

    <section id="closing" class="manifesto-block ts-brass">
      <h2>Section 6: Closing Declaration of Forward Faith</h2>
      <p>
        We step boldly into the frontier before us. Guided by Truth · Equity · Faith, we will shepherd land,
        law, and community toward renewal. The Faith Frontier Ecclesiastical Trust moves with God-centered
        momentum—ever building, ever stewarding, ever serving—so that New Jersey becomes a living testament to
        justice, mercy, and shared prosperity.
      </p>
    </section>
